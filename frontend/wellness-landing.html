Privacy and safety

Your health data should feel held, not harvested.

LumaWell is designed around clear consent, local-first wellness notes, and simple controls for what gets synced to integrations.

01 Private by default

Mood notes and check-ins stay personal unless you choose to export or sync them.

02 Clear integrations

Connect Apple Health or Google Fit only for the signals you approve, like sleep or steps.

03 Gentle notifications

Reminders can be quiet, scheduled, or paused so the app supports your rhythm.

Care note

LumaWell supports everyday wellbeing and habit building. It is not a medical device and does not replace advice from a doctor, therapist, or licensed healthcare professional.

Questions

Small answers before you begin.

Do I need experience with meditation?

No. LumaWell starts with short, guided sessions and simple breathing prompts for beginners.

Can I use it for fitness tracking too?

Yes. The app supports gentle movement, walks, mobility, strength routines, and recovery-aware progress.

Does LumaWell connect to health apps?

The product concept supports Apple Health and Google Fit integrations for movement, sleep, and recovery signals.

Is my wellness data private?

Wellness data should be treated as sensitive. LumaWell is designed around private-by-default tracking and clear user control.
